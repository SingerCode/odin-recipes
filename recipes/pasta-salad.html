Stormsong Pasta Salad

Description

Beat the heat of summer and cool down with this timeless recipe straight from the kitchens of Stormsong. This cold pasta salad is perfect after a long day of work.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Stormsong Valley Ranch (or equivalent)
  • 1/2 lb (250g) bacon, diced
  • 2 chicken breast (~1 lb/.5kg)
  • 1 cup (225oz) sour cream or mayo. Or greek yogurt. Or a mix of all.
  • 1/2 bunch of spring onions
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 cup frozen peas (don't thaw)
  • 1 can black olives, sliced
  • About 8oz of cherry tomatoes, cut in half
  • 16oz (550g) of pasto of choice, cooked al dente

Instructions:

  1. Dice the bacon small, then fry up crispy. Reserve the fat.
  2. Dice the chicken, then fry in the bacon fat. Alternatively, boil, or bake, and shred to save calories.
  3. Mix ranch packet with sour cream (or alternate).
  4. Shred carrot, cut onions, olives and tomatoes.
  5. Mix pasta, veggies (including frozen peas) and meats into the sauce packet.
  6. Mix to coat.
  7. Chill at least 1 hour.
  8. Enjoy!
